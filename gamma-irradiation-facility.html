Gamma Irradiation Facility

Home / Gamma Irradiation Facility

Gamma innovation, sterilization perfected

Pioneering rapid, advanced gamma irradiation sterilization - delivering safe, reliable, and validated decontamination solutions for industries

PSIL proudly operates one of India’s fastest and most advanced Gamma irradiation facilities, offering state-of-the-art gamma radiation sterilization services to a diverse range of industries. Our cutting-edge plant is meticulously engineered to deliver efficient and reliable decontamination, ensuring they meet the highest standards of safety and hygiene.

Utilizing the proven benefits of gamma radiation sterilization, our process effectively destroys microorganisms by disrupting their DNA at the molecular level. This “cold” sterilization technique is ideal for heat and moisture-sensitive products, allowing sterilization without compromising product integrity. Our facility is designed to accommodate various industries with stringent requirements including healthcare, pharmaceuticals, food packaging, and agricultural sectors.

Quality assurance is a cornerstone of our Gamma Irradiation Facility. We rigorously implement Gamma irradiation sterilization validation processes to verify that our sterilization services consistently achieve the required sterility assurance level (SAL). Through careful monitoring and adherence to regulatory standards, we guarantee the safety, efficacy, and reliability of every batch processed at our plant.

Committed to delivering superior service at competitive market-leading prices, PSIL emphasizes customer satisfaction and operational excellence. By leveraging advanced technology and a skilled team, we support our clients in maintaining product safety and compliance—making us a trusted partner in gamma irradiation sterilization solutions across India.
